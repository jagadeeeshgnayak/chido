Fresh Vegetables: Auction Price

  • Auction – Daily we go to the nearest market, do the auction based on the vegetable type and orders qty.
  • Grading and Packing – Daily we do the first quality grading and then we do the packing.
  • Storage – We store the vegetable in cold storage to maintain the freshness.
  • Delivery – We do the delivery by using cold chain vehicles to maintain the freshness.
    1. If the order has been placed before 12PM then we do the delivery on the same day evening.
    2. If the order has been placed after 12PM then we do the delivery next day morning.
  • Payments – We accept the payments at the time of delivery, any mode of payment will be accepted.

Fresh Milk

  • Milk Collection Process - Our staff will go to formers place, do the health check for the cow, and then start milking through automation process without using hands.
  • Quality Check - We do the regular quality checks.
  • Hygienic Packing – Milk is packed in glass bottle without touching by hands.
  • Hygienic Cleaning – We do hygiene cleaning of the bottle using sterilized semi-automatic bottle cleaning machines.
  • Spot Cooling– The milk is cooled to 4°C immediately in our cold chain vehicle to prevent bacterial growth and loss of nutrients.
  • Delivery - The milk is moved from our facilities to your doorstep in continuous cold chain vehicles, delivery will do in the morning before 7.30 AM and in the evening before 8 PM.
